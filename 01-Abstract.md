Abstract

Enrollment management in educational institutions involves the publishing of admission information, major setup, application review, and admission notices, and traditional offline processes often suffer from delayed information delivery, fragmented workflows, and difficult statistics. To improve the standardization and efficiency of enrollment management, this thesis designs and implements an enrollment management system based on Spring Boot. The system adopts a B/S architecture with front‑end and back‑end separation. The back end uses Spring Boot and MyBatis‑Plus to organize business logic, the front end uses Vue.js to build management interfaces, and the data layer relies on MySQL for centralized storage and management of enrollment data. The system serves administrators and students, covering admission announcement management, major information management, application management, admission notice management, student information management, system configuration, and file upload functions, and it ensures operational security and traceability through access control and logging. Testing results show that the system can stably support enrollment workflows and meet the daily management needs of educational institutions.

Keywords: enrollment management system; Spring Boot; MyBatis‑Plus; MySQL; access control
